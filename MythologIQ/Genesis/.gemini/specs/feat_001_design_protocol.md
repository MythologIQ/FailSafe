## 1. Context & Goal

The User identified "The Design Phase" as the most important step: bringing structure to the "Spark." currently, we have `Specs` (Technical), but we lack `Blueprints` (Conceptual).

### [NEW] [Blueprint Template]

- **File**: `g:\MythologIQ\Genesis\Design Documents\_TEMPLATE_BLUEPRINT.md`
- **Purpose**: A comprehensive "Product Requirements Document" (PRD) to validate business viability.
- **Sections**:
  - **1. Strategic Core (DeepWisdom)**: Problem, Value, Competitive Landscape, KPIs.
  - **2. Identity Framework (TACHES)**:
    - **T**arget & Trouble (Audience/Pain)
    - **A**tmosphere & Attitude (Tone/Vibe)
    - **C**ore Content (Key Messages)
    - **H**ierarchy & Layout (Structure)
    - **E**xperience & Actions (UX/CTA)
    - **S**tyle & Specifics (Aesthetics/Metaphor)
  - **3. Strategic Alignment**: Roadmap fit.

**Goal**: Create a dedicated "Ideation Layer" that precedes the "Specification Layer." This ensures that the "Why" and the "Vision" are solidified before we talk about files and functions.

## 4. The Change Delta (Plan)

### [NEW] [Genesis Lifecycle Map]

We are adopting the **BMAD 4-Phase Methodology** as our Macro-Architecture:

1.  **Analysis** -> _Genesis Design Phase_ (Blueprints)
2.  **Planning** -> _Genesis Spec Phase_ (Specs)
3.  **Solutioning** -> _Genesis Architect Mode_ (Implementation Plans)
4.  **Implementation** -> _Genesis Builder Mode_ (Code)

### [NEW] [Ideation Workflow]

- **File**: `g:\MythologIQ\Genesis\.agent\workflows\ideation_session.md`
- **Purpose**: A "Creative Intelligence Suite" (CIS) workflow for Phase 1.
