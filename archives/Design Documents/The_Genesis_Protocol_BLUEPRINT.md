## I. Strategic Core (DeepWisdom)

_The Business Logic. Why are we building this?_

### 1. Problem Statement

- **The Pain**: Engagement with AI often leads to "Drift," recursive loops, and "getting in our own way." The User's eagerness and the Agent's sycophancy can create negative value (waste).
- **The Cost**: Time, energy, and trust lost to non-deterministic outcomes.

### 2. Value Proposition

- **The Gain**: A "Net Positive Result" from every interaction.
- **The Mechanism**: Strategic, rigid, deterministic protocols (Guard Rails) that do not depend on significant external resources.
- **The Goal**: "Pure Productivity" and "Quality Code."

### 3. Competitive Landscape

- **The Constraint**: Must function _without_ heavy external dependencies (Standalone Excellence).
- **The Edge**: Unlike standard "Chat" interfaces, this system protects both entities (Agent and User) from their own biases.

### 4. Success Metrics (KPIs)

- [ ] **Zero Hallucination Loops**: Agent never repeats a failed error > 2 times.
- [ ] **Net Positive Output**: Every session yields a committed Artifact or Code.
- [ ] **Protocol Adherence**: 100% compliance with "The Sentiment Neutrality Rule."

### 5. Strategic Alignment (Genesis)

- **Roadmap Fit**: This is the **Operating System** of the entire repository.

### 6. Future Vision (The Horizon)

- **The Medium**: A small form-factor UI (Browser Extension / Sidebar).
- **The Value**: "Genesis" becomes a portable layer that sits _on top_ of Antigravity, enforcing the Constitution in real-time, regardless of the workspace.
